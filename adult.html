Adult Volleyball

Gather your friends, form a team and stay active in one of our many adult volleyball leagues, classes, tournaments, and club. Whether it’s casual or competitive, there’s always an opportunity to keep fit and have fun! #joinus

Our goal is to provide opportunities year-round for indoor, grass, and beach adult volleyball at all levels. We will help players develop to their highest level to provide the perfect balance of social, health and competition.

Find a Player: Looking for a player to fill our your league roster or sub? Check out our Facebook Community Page at NVVA Adult Volleyball.

About

Level Descriptions:

Level B: Consists of teams with a very basic/beginner understanding of the game and rules but few developed skills.

Level BB: Consists of teams with a good knowledge of the game and rules. Skill levels range from beginner/interediate to proficient veteran. All players understand rotational sequence, offensive concepts (i.e., 5-1, 6-2) and defensive positions but are working to solidify those skills.

Level A: Consists of teams with a solid knowledge of the game and rules. Skill levels range from advanced Intermediate to experienced. All players understand rotational sequence, offensive concepts (i.e., 5-1, 6-2) and defensive positions. Game and player strategies are well understood (i.e., running offensive plays, identifying opponent weaknesses, reading opponents plays) but may still be in refinement.

Level AA: Consists of teams with a superior knowledge of the game and rules. Skill levels range from Experienced to Professionally Trained Athletes. All players understand rotational sequence, offensive concepts (i.e., 5-1, 6-2, etc.) and defensive positions. Game and player strategies are well understood (i.e., running offensive plays, identifying opponent weaknesses, reading opponents plays during volley, etc.).

NVVA will provide a year-round opportunity to socialize and compete at the right age group and level of play. We will reward players with the recognition of their accomplishments.
