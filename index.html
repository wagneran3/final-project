Our Mission

We believe in creating a clear path for volleyball enthusiasts so they can navigate through the fun, lifelong, and sometimes complex experiences of volleyball. We provide the best facilities, environment, and programs that offer a roadmap to support and guide each player in the growth and achievement of their goals. We bring a better way to experience volleyball so each athlete can achieve excellence in this sport and throughout life.

Virginia Volleyball Center

The Virginia Volleyball Center (VVC) is the home of NVVA. This 55,000 square foot volleyball facility is equipped with all the tools to develop athletes and host the best volleyball tournaments and events.

  • Full-time staff
  • full-size indoor volleyball courts
  • LCD TVs on each court
  • Indoor beach volleyball court
  • Fully stocked weight room
  • Comfortable lobby area - Free wi-fi
  • Classroom for up to 50 people
