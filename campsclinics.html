NVVA offers multiple levels of private training to fit your athlete's specific skill level. Our Advanced, Advanced/Intermediate, Youth Development, and Beach Volleyball Lessons cover the full range of skill levels.

Private training is available for individuals, but also for multiple athletes and groups. As the only club in Northern Virginia with an indoor sand volleyball court, NVVA and our coaches are able to offer private beach lessons to our athletes year round. Also available are Strength & Conditioning lessons in our fully stocked Weight Room.

NVVA has the most qualified and experienced coaches to fit every need and every skill level. Our goal is to help athletes develop their full potential and private lessons are a great personalized way of achieving this for our players!

Youth Development

Youth Development coaches are coaches that are experts in teaching the basics of the game. We recommend youth development coaches for beginner athletes that want to learn the basics of the game.

Kory Borde

Coach Kory Borde has been a Head Coach for Club volleyball at Northern Virginia Volleyball Association since 2013. Coach Borde has Coached Club 14’s, 15’s and 16’s. He also Coaches Galaxy League ages 10-14 and runs the skills portion of the league to help ensure the development of all the younger athletes in the program.

Coach Borde has over 10 years of coaching experience. He has coached for NVVA as a head coach for Club, Galaxy, Volley Juniors and Loudoun Youth Volleyball leagues. He has also coached at Briar Woods High School as the Freshman Volleyball Head Coach 2013-2014 season where he lead the team to an 18-2 record and brought home the first volleyball trophy to the school. In addition to coaching volleyball, Coach Borde has coached soccer with Jefferson Youth Soccer Association in the American Youth Soccer Organization and Loudoun Soccer, Football with the AYFL and Baseball with DLL. He also has organized and supervised a number of volleyball tournaments, clinics and camps.
