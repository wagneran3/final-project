Volley Juniors

Volley Juniors is a program for boys and girls, ages 7-12 who would like to learn the fundamentals of the sport in skills appropriate, yet relaxed, environment. The season consists of a series of 8 classes on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM. Each class is divided into sections of volleyball skills training and sections of game-play. Games used will teach volleyball movement using a game called Smashball. This program is a good precursor to our Galaxy League

*Volley Juniors will return this Spring! Stay tuned for program updates and the reopening of the facility.

Youth Academy

Youth Academy is a program for boys and girls, ages 7-12 who would like to learn the fundamentals of the sport in skills appropriate, yet relaxed, environment. Players are placed on the court best suited for them according to their current skills and athleticism. The season consists of a series of 7 classes and each class is divided into 30 minutes of volleyball skills training and 1 hour of play. This program is a good precursor to our Galaxy League.

Galaxy League

The Galaxy League program was established for players in middle school & high school ages 10-15. Players are chosen based on athletic and skill testing, then placed on a team with participants with similar testing scores and age.

Galaxy will host up to 12 teams during each session. Each season will last around 8 weeks and teams will practice twice a week at the VVC – Mondays and Fridays.

Class Location: Virginia Volleyball Center

Address: 45686 Oakbrook Court, Suite 140. Sterling, VA 20166

Phone: 571-313-1748

Email: support@nvva.com

Tryouts

Athletes interested in playing multiple sessions must attend a tryout for each session.

Practices

Teams practice in one of two sessions. On Mondays, the first session will practice from 5:00pm – 6:30pm and on Fridays from 5:00pm – 6:30pm . The second session practices Mondays 6:30pm – 8:00pm and Fridays 6:30pm – 8:00pm.

End-of-Session Tournament

Each 8-week session will culminate with an end of season tournament at the VVC playing against other Galaxy teams.

The first session will compete in the morning wave of the tournament (8am - 1pm), and the second session will play in the afternoon wave (1pm - 6pm).

Beach Galaxy League

The Beach Galaxy League program was established for middle school players 10-14 who want to learn the beach game. There are no assessments as players will play 4v4 since the beach court is a little smaller than the indoor court.

Each season will last around 8 weeks and teams will practice twice a week at the VVC – Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Class Location: Virginia Volleyball Center

Address: 45686 Oakbrook Court, Suite 140. Sterling, VA 20166

Phone: 571-313-1748

Email: support@nvva.com

Tryouts

There are no assessments, but classes are limited to 12 players

Practices

Practices are Tuesdays and Thursdays from 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM. Tuesday practices will emphasize a specific skill such as serving, setting, passing or hitting, and Thursdays will be an opportunity to practice that skill in game play.

End-of-Session Tournament

Each 8-week session will culminate with an end of season on the last Thursday of the season. The players will play a tournament style called Monarch of the Beach.
